SCHOOL TOUR

School Tour

19/05/2018

SCHOOL TOUR TO VANDALOOR

Welcome to all the winners of various activities that were conducted during the Academic year. Addressing on the occasion, the Chief Guest lauded and praised the School for raising the Educational Standard in the City. Tamil and English skits were performed too. The Tamil play was about a legendary Tamil Woman – ‘Kannagi’ – from the Tamil Epic Silapathikaram, while the English play was from the famous fairy tale - Cinderella. Both were very well appreciated. to an end.
