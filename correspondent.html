OUR CORRESPONDENT
Dear Parent,

It is my pleasure to reach you through this note. I first thank you for choosing M.K.M. Mat. Hr. Sec. School for your wards education. I would like to make you realise that the main aim of the school is to produce smart and confident citizens of india who would go out to the world and makes us proud! And also to develop the virtue of good human relationship based on sensitivity, tolerance and good will.

The objective of the school is to encourage an attitude of positive response to the persistent demands of the changing society, yet firmly upholding a belief in basic values and standards of life.

It helps to identify, recognize and encourage talents and to create a suitable environment, where in these creative capacities of the individual will be further developed. The school also caters in accepting responsibilities for identifying and satisfying the physical, intellectual, spiritual and social needs of each individual member of the school community and provide him / her with the skills to become worthy and contributing citizen of the world tomorrow.

M.K.M. strongly believes that, it is character that makes a man perfect. So I make my word that your child under our guidance will definitely come out in blooming colours to face the fast growing competitive world with upgraded knowledge and courage for all his/her endeavors and challenges in future.
WE WISH HIM / HER 'ALL THE VERY BEST'.
M.KANTHIMATHY CORRESPONDENT
